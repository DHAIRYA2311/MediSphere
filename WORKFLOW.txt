OFFLINE CONSULTATION WORKFLOW (OPD)
Step-by-step flow
Patient books OFFLINE appointment
↓
Appointment status = Confirmed
↓
Patient arrives → consultation happens
↓
Appointment status = Completed
↓
Invoice AUTO-GENERATED
↓
Payment (Cash / UPI / Card)
↓
END (unless admitted)
Auto-create bill ONLY after consultation ends



VIDEO / ONLINE CONSULTATION WORKFLOW

This is almost same, with one small difference.

Step-by-step flow
Patient books VIDEO consultation
↓
Appointment status = Confirmed
↓
Payment REQUIRED before session (recommended)
↓
Video consultation happens
↓
Appointment status = Completed
↓
Invoice auto-generated (or marked Paid)
↓
END



OFFLINE CONSULTATION → WARD ADMISSION (IPD)

This is the most important flow you asked about.

Correct medical workflow
Offline consultation completed
↓
Doctor decides: Admission required
↓
Doctor clicks: "Admit to Ward"
↓
Ward & bed allocated
↓
Patient status = Admitted
↓
NEW billing cycle starts (IPD)
